---
title: Settings
description: Configure sending identity and email service providers.
---

Settings has two areas:

- **General** stores the team mailing address used for sender compliance and unsubscribe-related email requirements.
- **ESP** configures one or more email service providers and marks one as the default.

SendLit supports custom SMTP, SendGrid, Mailgun, Postmark, Amazon SES, Resend, and other custom providers. Provider credentials are write-only: stored secrets are never returned by the API.

Use **Test connection** after adding a provider. A provider must be configured before broadcasts or active sequences can deliver mail.
