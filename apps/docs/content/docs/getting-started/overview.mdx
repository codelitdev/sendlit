title: Overview
description: The main SendLit concepts.
---

SendLit helps teams compose and deliver email through four main resources:

- **Contacts** are subscribers. Contacts can be segmented with tags, email filters, subscription status, and signup date.
- **Templates** are reusable email layouts.
- **Broadcasts** are one-time sends to a filtered audience.
- **Sequences** are automated multi-step email flows.

Before sending mail in production, configure the team's sending provider in settings. Without a team-level provider, SendLit will not send campaign mail.

![SendLit sign-in screen](/screenshots/sign-in.png)

Sign in with email OTP, then choose the team you want to work in. Every team has its own contacts, templates, automations, sending provider, API keys, and quota.
